What is HoloCure?

HoloCure is an unofficial fan-made game. You can enjoy all content completely.

Features

  • Unlock and play with 29 Hololive characters through Gacha (in-game currency only).
  • Unique skills and special attacks for every character
  • Unlock dozens of different weapons and passive items
  • Hololive song remixes created for this game by Eufrik
  • Survive against classic Hololive characters and mascots

AVAILABLE LANGUAGE OPTIONS

  • English (英語)
  • Japanese (日本語)
